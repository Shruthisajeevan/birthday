✦   a little confession

Okay — I have to be honest with you.

I'm probably not your first wish today. Someone else got there before me — maybe at midnight, maybe the second your eyes opened. And honestly? That's okay.

But here is what that person didn't do —
they didn't spend an entire week thinking about this moment. Planning it. Rewriting it at 1am. Scrapping three different ideas. Wanting so badly for this to feel worthy of you.

And then — somehow — today came, and I panicked. Not because I forgot you. Never that. Never once.

I forgot the date because I was too busy thinking about you in every other moment. And I know that sounds like an excuse — but it is the most honest thing I have.
